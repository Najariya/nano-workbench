Welcome to Nano Workbench

A private AI powered by Google's Gemini Nano, running on your device inside Chrome — no accounts, no API keys. Your text and notes stay local.

It reads what you're viewing

Open any page or email and tap Summarize, ask a question, or pull out to‑dos. Long documents are summarized in parts automatically.

Right‑click superpowers

Select text and right‑click “Ask Nano”, or right‑click any image to extract its text. Use 🎙 Speak for voice input.

◈

Setting up on-device AI

Powered by Google's Gemini Nano, running locally inside Chrome — not the cloud Gemini.

Downloading Gemini Nano… 0%

Once downloaded (~2 GB, one time), the AI works offline — the text you summarize, ask about, and your saved notes stay on your device. Gemini Nano is a small model: great for summaries and drafts, but it can be wrong, so verify anything important. Note: the optional voice‑input feature uses Chrome's speech recognition, which may process audio in the cloud.
